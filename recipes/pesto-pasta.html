Pesto Protein Pasta

70+ g protein

Ingredients

  • Free to Eat beet pasta
  • Vegan canned pesto (1/2 jar)
  • Drizzle of tahini and hemp seeds

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the pasta in salted water
  2. When al dente, pour pesto sauce over it and stir
  3. Top with a drizzle of tahini and a sprinkle of hemp seeds-- this will give it a really nice texture
