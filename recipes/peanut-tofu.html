Spicy Peanut Tofu

70+ g protein

Ingredients

  • 1 block of Nasoya super firm tofu
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Rice for serving

Sauce:

  • Fly by Jing chili oil
  • Mighty Sesame Co. tahini
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin or rice vinegar
  • Sugar or sweetener of choice
  • Garlic
  • Onion powder
  • Scallions

Instructions:

  1. Coat tofu with some cornstarch and salt
  2. Fry in some olive oil until crispy
  3. Meanwhile, mix the sauce by combining all ingredients to taste
  4. Once the tofu is crispy and golden brown, pour the sauce over and let coat for a minute or two. Then serve over rice.
