Caviar/Beluga Lentils

50+ g protein per cup

Ingredients

  • Black caviar lentils (can sub beluga or French green lentils)
  • Diced onion
  • Vegan butter

Instructions:

  1. Preper the lentils with butter in the water
  2. When done, stir in carmelized onions
  3. Serve with a (unsweetened) vegan sour yogurt
